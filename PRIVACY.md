# Privacy Policy for AetherTranslate

Last updated: June 14, 2026

AetherTranslate is committed to protecting user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled by the AetherTranslate Companion Chrome Extension and its associated translation backend.

## 1. Information We Collect and Process
* **Website Content**: The extension only reads the text content of the active tab when you explicitly click the "Translate Page" button. This text is sent securely via HTTPS to your hosted translation backend server to perform simplified Chinese and Pinyin annotation.
* **No Storage**: The webpage text is processed entirely in memory, translated, and returned to your browser. **We do not store, log, track, or save any webpage content or translation history.**
* **Local Settings**: User preferences (such as display layout and toggle states) are stored locally on your device via Chrome's local storage APIs and are never transmitted to any external server.

## 2. Information Sharing and Disclosure
We do not sell, trade, rent, or share user data with third parties. No personally identifiable information (such as IP addresses, emails, or account details) is collected or processed.

## 3. Policy Changes
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

## 4. Contact
For any questions regarding this privacy policy, please open an issue in the project repository.
